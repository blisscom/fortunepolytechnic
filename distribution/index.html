VISION STATEMENT

To build a technological driven institution in an excellent learning environment supported with well-equipped laboratories and highly skilled manpower with practical experience in chosen fields of knowledge in order to make a difference in the lives of graduands.

MISSION STATEMENT

To be a World class institution essential for the development of the next generation of leaders that will empower innovation for self-reliance and arrow a diversified economy that will foster a stable and vibrant social structure for global leaders

PHILOSOPHY

The Philosophy of the Polytechnics is reflected in the provision of courses of instruction, and research in technology, applied science, environmental studies, commerce, management and other fields of study aimed at producing sub-professionals who will be capable of performing at an acceptable standard for development and adopting appropriate techniques at problem-solving to the society.
